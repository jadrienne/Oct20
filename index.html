As a small business owner, I believe that each piece should be unique and OAK (one-of-a-kind) so I create my jewelry by using different patterns, colors, shapes, and themes.

In terms of ideating designs, I take inspiration from mostly just my beads and themes that l've thought of in my head. When I first started in 2019, I used to go on Pinterest or even take a look at other jewelry creators' creations for inspiration when I create. In the long run, I realized that it is simply copying others' ideas and this in turn displays little to no originality and creativity of your own; hence, I decided to just work with what I have such as my imagination; various themes, movies, and songs.

I try my best to design and create my pieces intuitively and uniquely for Bunnytears to stand out.
